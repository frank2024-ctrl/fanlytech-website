A closer look at two projects.

Fintech

Northgate Financial: One dashboard, replacing three legacy tools.

timeline9 weeks
team4 engineers
stackReact, Node, PostgreSQL

// the_problem

Northgate's analysts were pulling data across three disconnected internal tools every morning, manually reconciling numbers before client meetings — a process prone to errors and taking up to two hours daily per analyst.

// our_approach

We consolidated all three data sources into a single real-time dashboard with role-based views, built on a new API layer that pulled directly from Northgate's trading systems.

// the_result

Analysts now get live data with zero manual reconciliation. The dashboard has become the primary tool for over 4,000 daily users across the firm.

63%faster page load vs. legacy tools
~2 hrssaved per analyst, per day
4,200daily active users
99.98%uptime since launch
"Fanlytech didn't just build what we asked for — they questioned our workflow first, and the dashboard we ended up with was better than what we originally scoped." — VP of Operations, Northgate Financial
Healthcare

Cascade Health: A patient portal built for compliance and ease of use.

timeline12 weeks
team5 engineers
stackNext.js, AWS, HIPAA-compliant infra

// the_problem

Cascade's patients had no way to book appointments or message providers online, driving a high volume of phone-only scheduling and a persistent no-show rate.

// our_approach

We built a HIPAA-compliant portal with online booking, automated reminders, and secure provider messaging — designed to be usable by patients across a wide age range.

// the_result

Missed appointments dropped meaningfully in the first quarter after launch, and the portal now handles the majority of new scheduling volume.

31%reduction in missed appointments
18,000+active patient accounts
72%of bookings now self-service
100%HIPAA compliance audit pass
"The team treated compliance as a first-class requirement, not an afterthought. That mattered enormously for a healthcare product." — Director of Digital Health, Cascade Health Network
