// faq

Pricing questions.

Is the project price really fixed?

Yes. After scoping, you get a fixed quote in writing. If new work comes up mid-project, we'll always confirm cost with you before proceeding — no surprise invoices.

Can I cancel a support plan anytime?

Monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. Annual plans are billed upfront but can be prorated and refunded for unused months if needed.

Do unused engineering hours roll over?

Up to 5 unused hours roll over to the following month on Partner and Dedicated plans.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer and all major credit cards. Project work is typically split 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.

Still not sure which plan fits?

Tell us about your project and budget — we'll recommend the right starting point.
